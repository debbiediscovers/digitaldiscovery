Due Diligence

We provide independent, commercially-focused assessment of digital assets for investment funds. Our analysis covers traffic quality, acquisition economics, technical infrastructure, and growth runway—providing clarity on digital strengths, risks, and opportunities.

Who We Work With

Digital Discovery partners with established enterprises and founder-led growth companies with the ambition and the capacity to scale profitably.

We work directly with marketing and management teams to build in-house capability, implement advanced marketing acquisition frameworks, and design the underlying systems and architecture required for sustainable growth. We measure performance and results rigorously and aggressively.

We also advise investment funds on commercial due diligence and post-acquisition value creation, bringing clarity to growth assumptions and strengthening operating performance at the portfolio level.

We are deliberately selective with our clients. Our engagements are structured as retained, long-term partnerships with organisations that prioritise strategic depth, analytical rigour and disciplined execution, where performance is sustained and measurable, not not unsustainable or short-lived.
